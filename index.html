Meet the team

The L & J behind the work

Two hardworking locals who started L&J to help their neighbors and build something of their own.

Layton Turner

Co-Owner

I started L&J because I've always liked working with my hands and helping people get things done, whether that's a fresh-cut lawn, a cleared-out barn, or an extra set of hands on a job site. I take pride in showing up, working hard, and leaving folks better off than I found them. Down the road, I'd love to see L&J grow into a name people in Joplin trust first when they need honest, reliable help, with a full crew and the equipment to take on bigger jobs every year.

Jaymes Jackson

Co-Owner

For me, L&J is about building something real, putting in honest work and never turning down a job just because it's tough. I like the variety of it: one day it's lawn care, the next it's hauling or labor on a build. Every job is a chance to prove we're dependable. My goal is to grow L&J into a steady, respected business that creates jobs in our community and gives people a company they can count on for years to come.
